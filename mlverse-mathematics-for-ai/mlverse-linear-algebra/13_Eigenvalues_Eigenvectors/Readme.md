# Eigenvalues and Eigenvectors

## Overview

Eigenvalues and Eigenvectors are among the most important concepts in Linear Algebra.

They reveal the hidden structure of matrices and power many modern AI systems.

Applications include:

- Principal Component Analysis (PCA)
- Singular Value Decomposition (SVD)
- Computer Vision
- Recommender Systems
- Graph Neural Networks
- Spectral Clustering
- Transformers
- Scientific Computing

---

## Why Learn Eigenvalues and Eigenvectors?

When a matrix transforms a vector, most vectors change both:

- Direction
- Magnitude

However, some special vectors only change magnitude.

These are called Eigenvectors.

The scaling factor is called the Eigenvalue.
