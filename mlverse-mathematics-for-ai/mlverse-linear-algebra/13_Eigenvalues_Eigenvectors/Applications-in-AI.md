# Eigenvalues and Eigenvectors in AI

Eigenvalues and Eigenvectors are everywhere in Artificial Intelligence.

---

## Principal Component Analysis (PCA)

PCA computes eigenvectors of covariance matrices.

These become principal components.

---

## Computer Vision

Used in:

- Face Recognition
- Eigenfaces
- Object Detection

---

## Deep Learning

Eigenvalues help analyze:

- Gradient Flow
- Optimization Stability
- Hessian Matrices

---

## Spectral Clustering

Uses graph eigenvectors to discover clusters.

---

## Recommendation Systems

Matrix factorization relies on eigen-analysis.

---

## Graph Neural Networks

Graph Laplacian eigenvectors reveal graph structure.

---

## Natural Language Processing

Embedding spaces often leverage spectral methods.

---

## Robotics

State-space systems use eigenvalues for stability analysis.

---

## Control Systems

Eigenvalues determine system behavior.

---

## Scientific Computing

Many simulations rely on eigen-decomposition.
