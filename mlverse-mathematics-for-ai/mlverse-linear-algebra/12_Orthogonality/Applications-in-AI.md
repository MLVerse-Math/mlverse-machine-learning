# Orthogonality in Artificial Intelligence

Orthogonality is everywhere in AI.

---

## Principal Component Analysis (PCA)

Principal Components are orthogonal.

This prevents redundant information.

---

## Singular Value Decomposition (SVD)

Orthogonal matrices form the foundation of SVD.

---

## Deep Learning

Orthogonal initialization helps:

- Stable gradients
- Faster convergence
- Better training

---

## Computer Vision

Orthogonal transformations preserve geometric properties.

---

## Signal Processing

Signals are decomposed into orthogonal components.

Examples:

- Fourier Transform
- Wavelets

---

## Natural Language Processing

Embedding spaces often use orthogonal representations.

---

## Transformers

Attention mechanisms benefit from orthogonal feature representations.

---

## Recommendation Systems

Orthogonal latent factors improve interpretability.

---

## Robotics

Orthogonal rotation matrices describe motion and orientation.
